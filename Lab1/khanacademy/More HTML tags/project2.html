Fluffy Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 ½ cups
Milk 1 ¼ cups
Egg 1
Baking Powder 3 ½ tsp

Steps:

  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter to form pancakes.
  4. Cook until golden on both sides.

Creamy Garlic Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Garlic 3 cloves
Heavy Cream 1 cup
Parmesan Cheese ½ cup

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté garlic in a pan until fragrant.
  3. Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in pasta and cheese, then serve warm.
